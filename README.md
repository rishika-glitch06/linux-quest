Welcome to **Linux Quest**! 👋

This isn't just another Linux notes repository.

It's my personal journey of learning Linux—from understanding the fundamentals to exploring advanced concepts, building shell scripts, solving real-world challenges, and documenting everything I learn along the way.

Instead of letting my notes stay buried in a notebook, I decided to turn them into something that anyone can learn from.

Whether you're taking your first step into Linux or revising concepts before an interview, I hope this repository makes your learning journey a little easier (and maybe a little more fun too 😄).
